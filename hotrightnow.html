Fall trends 2021

Find out what's trendy and what should be hidden in your closet this fall.

By Dominyka Muraskaite

CELEBRATING NEW YEAR'S IN AUTUMN

Fall season is a time for interesting layering, dynamic textures and a richer, warmer color palette. F. Scott Fitzgerald wrote in The Great Gatsby that “life starts all over again when it gets crisp in the fall.” Fall has always felt this way to me: a fresh start, even as we are heading toward the end of the year. We start fresh in our jobs, schools, maybe even relationships and friendships. It’s like New Year’s because you create new goals, set a positive, different mindset in order to become successful in a field that you’re good at or maybe you decide to start off autumn with trying something new, like a new hobby, new location to grab a cup of coffee, or even changing your qualification, who knows.

TRENDS

This fall, surprise surprise, welcomes you with new fashion trends that you can follow if you want to look fashionable and up-to-date. Since fall is known for fashion weeks (there’s one in Copenhagen, if you’re interested) and Halloween parties you will definitely see many ideas and inspiration for your outfits. This year there are many trends that I actually love and will be wearing and because of that I’ll let you in to the secrets of this autumn giving you advice on what to wear, how to style this season’s trends and where to find clothes and accessories that won’t break your bank.

EXPLORE THIS SEASON'S MUST HAVES

Eager to explore what the fashion world has to offer, I’ve gathered some of my favorite trends, all of which are totally wearable, easy to incorporate into your existing wardrobe, classic enough to roll around season after season and available at all price points. Here’s to a whole new season of fashion fun!

COZY SHACKET

In summer we wear denim jackets, in fall it’s time to bring out a soft, wool or fleecy shacket that will keep you warm and also fashionable since it’s a staple piece this season, which goes with anything that you have in your closet such as dresses, boots, leather pants, heels, you name it.

KNEE-HIGH BOOTS

Time for some rock n roll, baby! These bad boys will make you look fierce and feel like you own the whole world. I’m wearing them with a black turtle-neck sweater, plaid skirt and a leather jacket…

COLLARED JUMPER

Polo shirt + jumper= COLLARED JUMPER! What more could you ask for. It'll keep your warm but stylish at the same time without needing to put a lot of effort into your outfit. Some jumpers have buttons while some are V-neck and some are fitted while some are loose. Wear it to your friend's birthday paired with a skirt or save it for that special occasion,job interview,perhaps. Just throw on a blazer to make it more formal and you're good to go.

PLEATED MINI SKIRT

This piece is extremely versatile and should be in everyone's closet since it can be worn during any season,whether it's pouring rain or when it's sunny. Whether you're someone who enjoys classy style or are down for experimenting with new ways to wear old things, you'll find a ton of looks worth giving try. Cop one of these bad boys right now!
